# SAGACORE: DreamForge × MythOS
> Forge your ambitions. Shape your realm. Become the legend.
SAGACORE is an AI-powered gamified productivity platform that transforms real-world goals into immersive fantasy questlines. Instead of traditional to-do lists, users embark on dynamic adventures where goals become quests, accomplishments shape evolving realms, and progress is immortalized within a living codex.
Built with modern web technologies and AI-driven narrative generation, SAGACORE creates a unique experience that blends productivity, storytelling, and RPG progression into a single platform.

---

## Features

### DreamForge Engine
Transforms user goals into personalized quest campaigns.
* AI-generated questlines
* Dynamic quest difficulty scaling
* Goal decomposition into actionable tasks
* Multi-stage progression paths

### Narrative Engine
Creates evolving lore based on player actions.
* Procedural lore generation
* Dynamic codex chapters
* Quest completion narratives
* Persistent world storytelling

### World Architect
Choose or forge unique realms.
* Fantasy Realms
* Cyberpunk Grids
* Steampunk Kingdoms
* Custom AI-generated worlds

### Progression System

* XP & Leveling
* Character Ranks
* Quest Completion Tracking
* Realm Stability System
* Persistent Player Progress

### Memory Engine

* MongoDB-backed persistence
* Quest history tracking
* Lore archive storage
* Player state management
* Long-term progression continuity

## How It Works

1. Enter a real-world goal.
2. DreamForge converts it into a multi-stage quest campaign.
3. Complete roadmap tasks.
4. Gain XP and level up.
5. Unlock lore chapters and world evolution.
6. Store progression in MongoDB.
7. Continue building your personal legend.
